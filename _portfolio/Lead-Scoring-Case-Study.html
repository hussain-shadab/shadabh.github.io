--- title: "Lead Scoring Case Study" excerpt: "Built a logistic regression model to assign a lead score between 0 and 100 to each of the leads which can be used by the company to target potential leads." collection: portfolio date: 2019-03-03 --- Built a logistic regression model to assign a lead score between 0 and 100 to each of the leads which can be used by the company to target potential leads. A higher score would mean that the lead is hot, i.e. is most likely to convert whereas a lower score would mean that the lead is cold and will mostly not get converted.
